"Hi. I'm Madi and I'm addicted to creating."

"Hello, Madi."

A mid-western girl at heart, I relocated south to pursue my passion for making stuff. No really... just about anything. I love exploring texture, color, and limits. As a seventeen-year-old, SCAD was my place to explore. I fell in love with pattern design, screen printing, dye work, and textiles. After graduating with a B.F.A. in Fibers, I was hired at my (then) dream job creating installation artwork for Anthropologie.

Since, I have worked in product development, product design, graphic design, merchandising, marketing, photography, and interior styling. In my free time, I love to paint and spend time with family outdoors.
